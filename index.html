Web Fundamentals consists of 5 classes
for the Web Development course.

  1. A Bird’s-Eye View of the Web
  2. Web Architecture & Technologies
  3. Web APIs
  4. The Semantic Web & Linked Data
  5. Linked Data Publishing

I’m Ruben Verborgh, researcher and
professor of Semantic Web technology.

  • I conduct research at IDLab, UGent
    and the Decentralized Information Group, MIT.
    • I create solutions with Web technologies
      and measure their behavior.
  • I write papers and articles about this,
    and the occasional book.
  • I love the Web and I’ll share this passion with you.
    • Read more on Twitter and on my blog.

I occasionally teach courses like this one, and I do this for 3 main reasons.

  1. I like teaching.
    • You don’t really understand something
      until you can explain it to someone else.
  2. I want to spread the vision of the Web.
  3. I aim to attract talented people. Get in touch.
    • as project, internship, or job students
    • as master's thesis students
    • as future colleagues

These slides do not only include the truth, they also include my personal opinion.

  • Opinions are marked with the symbol.
    • The Web was invented in 1990.
    • Ted Nelson finds the Web rather limited.
    • The Web is the best invention of the past 30 years.
  • For the examination, know which is which.
    • Either reproduce my opinion (with arguments),
    • or state one of your own (with arguments).
