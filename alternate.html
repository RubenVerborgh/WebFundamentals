The Internet is a communication network between different machines in the world.

The Web is a layer of interlinked resources accessible through the Internet.

The World Wide Web has
a profound impact on the world.

  • It has changed the way we learn.
  • It has changed the way we communicate.
  • It has changed the way businesses work.
